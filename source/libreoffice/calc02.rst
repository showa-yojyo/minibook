Negative numbers
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To enter a negative number, either type a minus sign in front of the number or enclose the
number in parentheses, for example (1234). The result for both methods of entry is the same; for
example, –1234.
